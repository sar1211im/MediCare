Services at Healthy Heart Clinic

Explore the range of services offered by our health clinic.

Heart Health Checkup

A comprehensive examination to assess your heart's overall health, detect potential problems early, and monitor progress over time.

Heart Monitoring

Monitoring your heart rate, rhythm, and blood pressure regularly to identify any changes that may require immediate attention or further investigation.

Cholesterol Management

Working closely with our team of cardiologists to assess your cholesterol levels and develop a personalized treatment plan to lower your risk of heart disease.
